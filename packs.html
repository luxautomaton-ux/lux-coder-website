Playbook Anatomy

What is a Success Pack?

Every Success Pack contains structured code, prompts, and database schemas configured for specific business operations.

📅

Daily & Weekly Checklists

Step-by-step guides compiled into your dashboard. LANA prompts you to review pipeline stages, check lead contacts, and run structured weekly metrics analysis.

📊

CRM Schema & Pipelines

Pre-configured database layouts, custom stages, and contact categories mapped directly to your local offline CRM dashboard. No database programming needed.

✉️

Sales Outreach Templates

High-converting copy scripts, follow-up messages, proposal templates, and prompts customized for your target client profile and business niche.

⚙️

LANA Instructions & Rules

Specific system instructions and memory filters (`~/.lux/wiki/`) that direct LANA's reasoning, tone, response formats, and operations.
